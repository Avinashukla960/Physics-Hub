Our Mission

Fostering Intuition Through Interaction

At the University Department of Physics, we believe that the most effective way to learn is by doing. Textbooks and lectures can lay the groundwork, but true understanding and physical intuition are forged through active exploration and experimentation.

This platform was created to provide students with a virtual laboratory—a space to build, test, and visualize the fundamental principles of physics without the constraints of a physical lab. Our goal is to make learning physics more engaging, accessible, and intuitive for everyone.
